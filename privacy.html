Firme — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

Firme ("the app") is built to work almost entirely on your device. This policy explains, in plain language, what that means for your data.

The short version

We don't have a server. There's no account, no login, and no analytics SDK in this app. We don't know who you are, and we don't want to. The only outside party involved at all is Apple (for App Store purchases) and RevenueCat (which helps us manage subscriptions on Apple's behalf).

What's stored, and where

Your preferences — selected categories, favorited affirmations, theme, language, and notification schedule — are stored locally on your device using iOS's standard app storage (UserDefaults), shared between the Firme app and its lock-screen/home-screen widget via an App Group. None of this ever leaves your device or is transmitted to us.

Notifications

Daily affirmation notifications are scheduled locally on your device using Apple's notification framework. There is no push server — we never send anything to you remotely, and we have no way to know if or when a notification was delivered or opened.

Purchases and subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store and managed on our behalf by RevenueCat, a subscription infrastructure provider. RevenueCat receives purchase/transaction data needed to unlock premium features on your device — this is used solely for app functionality (verifying your entitlement) and is not linked to your identity or used for advertising. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details on their handling of this data.

What we don't do

  • We don't collect analytics or usage tracking of any kind.
  • We don't use advertising SDKs.
  • We don't require or ask for an account.
  • We don't sell or share data, because we don't have any to sell.

Children's privacy

Firme is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, regardless of age — the app simply doesn't have a mechanism to collect personal information in the first place.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the effective date above and, for material changes, note it in the app's "What's New" release notes.

Questions about this policy: support@example.com

Firme — Política de Privacidad

Fecha de vigencia: 17 de julio de 2026

(Spanish version — see above for the English original.)

Firme ("la app") funciona casi por completo en tu dispositivo. Esta política explica, en lenguaje simple, qué significa eso para tus datos.

En resumen

No tenemos servidor. No hay cuenta, ni inicio de sesión, ni SDK de analítica dentro de la app. No sabemos quién eres y no necesitamos saberlo. Los únicos terceros involucrados son Apple (para compras en la App Store) y RevenueCat (que nos ayuda a gestionar suscripciones en nombre de Apple).

Qué se guarda, y dónde

Tus preferencias —categorías seleccionadas, afirmaciones favoritas, tema, idioma y horario de notificaciones— se guardan localmente en tu dispositivo usando el almacenamiento estándar de iOS (UserDefaults), compartido entre la app Firme y su widget mediante un App Group. Nada de esto sale de tu dispositivo ni se nos transmite.

Notificaciones

Las notificaciones diarias se programan localmente en tu dispositivo. No existe un servidor de notificaciones push — nunca te enviamos nada de forma remota, y no tenemos manera de saber si una notificación fue entregada o abierta.

Compras y suscripciones

Las compras de suscripción son procesadas por Apple a través de la App Store y gestionadas en nuestro nombre por RevenueCat. RevenueCat recibe los datos de transacción necesarios para desbloquear funciones premium en tu dispositivo, únicamente para el funcionamiento de la app, sin vincularlos a tu identidad ni usarlos con fines publicitarios.

Privacidad infantil

Firme no está dirigida a menores de 13 años, y no recopilamos información de nadie, sin importar su edad — la app simplemente no tiene un mecanismo para recopilar información personal.

Contacto

Preguntas sobre esta política: support@example.com
